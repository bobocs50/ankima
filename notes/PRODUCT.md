# Product

Ankimax is a desktop application built specifically for existing Anki users.

It is not a flashcard app and it is not a replacement for Anki.

Ankimax acts as a capture, understanding, and card-creation layer that sits on top of a student's normal study workflow and connects directly into Anki.

## Core Idea

Students constantly encounter information they want to remember:

- lecture slides
- diagrams
- PDFs
- videos
- websites
- textbooks
- notes

Turning those materials into high-quality Anki cards is often slow, repetitive, and fragmented.

Current workflows usually look like:

Study Material → Screenshot → ChatGPT → Copy/Paste → Edit → Anki

Ankimax compresses this into a single workflow:

Capture → Understand → Create Card → Review → Save to Anki

## The Application

A lightweight floating HUD overlay. Users can:

- Capture screenshots
- Ask AI questions about captured material
- Generate AI-assisted flashcard drafts
- Edit cards before saving
- Apply reusable card presets and structures
- Push cards directly into Anki through AnkiConnect

## Product Philosophy

- AI assists learning rather than replacing learning
- Users remain responsible for card quality
- Understanding comes before memorization
- Every card is reviewed before export
- Consistency matters more than raw generation speed

## Positioning

Unlike PDF-to-Anki generators, Ankimax focuses on context preservation.

Unlike premade decks, Ankimax focuses on personal learning material.

Unlike generic AI flashcard tools, Ankimax focuses on structured card workflows, editing control, reusable presets, and direct integration into Anki.

## Long-Term Vision

Become the fastest and most intelligent interface between information a student encounters and the Anki cards they ultimately study.
